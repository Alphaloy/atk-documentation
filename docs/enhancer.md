## b.Fidelity 

Fidelity refers to the faithfulness and accuracy with which an audio system reproduces the original sound or signal. High fidelity, often abbreviated as "Hi-Fi," is a standard that aims to reproduce audio with minimal distortion and maximum accuracy. Fidelity implies that the audio playback system accurately reproduces the recorded or transmitted sound without introducing coloration, distortion, or alterations.
